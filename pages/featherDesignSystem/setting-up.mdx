  ## Get started as a Designer
  The Feather Design System (FDS) provides MPB with a shared language, facilitates cross-functional collaboration, and maintains design cohesion. These tools will help you get started and access our most up-to-date designs and standards.
  
  ## Connect to Feather Design System in Figma
  1. Install our font Suisse Int'l.
  2. Download Figma Desktop directly from the Figma Website.
  3. Once you have Figma on your computer, open the application and select “Log in with Google.” From there, enter your MPB email address and open Figma.
  4. As part of onboarding, you should have been invited to the MPB Product Design Team. You should have access to the Figma Feather libraries.
  5. When creating or opening an existing design file, you should see the FDS components and styles populate under the “Assets” tab in the left-hand column. Libraries include Feather Foundations (variables and styles), Feather Base UI components (main UI components), Feather Feature components (Platform specific feature components), Re.Sell Platform pages (contains all pages for Re.Sell as components as a source of truth), and Re.Serve Platform pages (contains all pages for Re.Serve as components as a source of truth).

  ## Figma library tips
  - Avoid detaching components when possible. Instead, use the component’s variant options or slot options within the component. If a component is not working as expected, reach out to jon.quayle@mpb.com.
  - Make sure your components stay up to date. You may see a notification in the bottom right corner of Figma when an update is available.
